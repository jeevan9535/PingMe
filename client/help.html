❓ Help & FAQ

➡ How to create a note?

Click the “+ Add Note” card in the main dashboard. You can then enter your note title, description, and an optional reminder.

➡ Where are my saved notes?

Your saved notes are available in the "Saved Notes" section. Use the sidebar to navigate and view them.

➡ Where can i see my total notes?

In the profile section you can see your total "Saved Notes"

➡ Forgotten User Name or Password

Click on the forgot Password and User name to Login again
